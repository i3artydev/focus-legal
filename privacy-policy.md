## 3. Data We Collect

### Account Data (cloud mode only)
When you sign in with Google, we receive from Google:
- Your Google account name
- Your email address
- Your Google profile photo URL
- A unique Google user identifier (UID)

This data is used solely to identify your account and is stored in Google Firebase.

### Task Data (cloud mode only)
Your tasks, labels, priorities, notes, and completion status are stored in Google Firebase Firestore to enable sync across devices. This data is associated with your Google UID.

### Device & Technical Data
We do not collect device identifiers, crash logs, analytics, or usage statistics.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services (cloud mode only)

When using cloud sync, we use the following Google services:

- **Firebase Authentication** — manages your sign-in via Google
- **Cloud Firestore** — stores your task data

These services are operated by Google LLC. Their privacy practices are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Firebase may collect certain technical data as described in [Firebase's data disclosure](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties except as required by the Firebase/Google services described above. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.

---

## 6. Data Retention & Deletion

**Local mode:** All data remains on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

**Cloud mode:** Your data is stored in Firebase for as long as you maintain an account. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from **Settings → Delete Account** within the app. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

You may also contact us at i3arty.dev@gmail.com to request data deletion.
